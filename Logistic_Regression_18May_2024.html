# Data Description

framingham dataset contains 4240 observations or rows and 16 variables or columns. It contains 15 attributes and 1 target variable. The goal of this study is to predict whether the patient has 10-year risk of coronary heart disease(CHD).

Variable descriptions male:binary variable indicating if patient is male(1) or female(0) age :age of patient in years education : years of patient education currentSmoker: variable that indicate if patients currently smoke(1) or no (0) cigsPerDay :Number of cigarettes smoke per day BPMeds :binary variable indicating if patient are on Blood pressure Medication(1) or not(0) prevalentStroke : patient succeptible of having stroke(1) or no(0) prevalentHyp: patient succeptible of having hypertension(1) or no(0) diabetes:binary variable that indicating if patient has diabetes(1) or no(0) totChol : cholesterol total rate sysBP : patient rate of systolic blood pressure patients diaBP :rate level diastolic blood pressure of patient BMI : Body Mass index of patient heartRate : heart rate of patient glucose:sugar rate in the blood TenYearCHD : binary variable indicating if the patient has 10-risk years of coronary heart disease(1)or not(0)

#data split
x_train,x_test,y_train,y_test =train_test_split(x,y,test_size=0.2,random_state=42)
#Initialization of Standardscaler
scaler=StandardScaler()
#standardization of features
x_train_scaled= scaler.fit_transform(x_train)
x_test_scaled = scaler.fit_transform(x_test)
#model fitting
logit_model=sm.GLM(y_train,x_train_scaled,family=sm.families.Binomial())
result=logit_model.fit()
